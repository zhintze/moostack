# Complete Ore Inventory: mooStack Modpack

This document catalogs every ore and ore-related material in the mooStack modpack.

---

| Mod | Ores | Key Materials | Duplicates With |
|-----|------|---------------|-----------------|
| Mekanism | Osmium, Tin, Lead, Uranium, Fluorite | Full processing chain (5x) | Lead, Uranium with IE |
| Immersive Engineering | Aluminum(Bauxite), Lead, Nickel, Silver, Uranium | Plates, rods, wires | Lead, Uranium with Mek |

## Notes

- Item IDs may vary slightly based on mod version
- Some items have alternative naming conventions (dust vs grit, nugget vs scrap)
- Create produces "crushed" variants instead of dusts
- Mekanism has the most complete ore processing chain (5x multiplication)
- IE has the most complete industrial components (plates, rods, wires)
- Silent Gear materials are dimension-specific (Azure Silver in End, Crimson Iron in Nether)
